Horizon (SVG)

2026-06-07 · SVG · vector art · static, no animation · 2.3 KB

The same horizon idea as the TIC-80 cart, but drawn in pure SVG. Gradient sky, striped sun, perspective grid — all rendered as vector primitives with a single linear gradient for the sky. The file is 2.3 KB, scales to any size without pixelation, and lives entirely in one self-contained .svg.

The two pieces are intentionally similar in composition but different in tooling. The pixel cart is hand-bit-pushed; the SVG is geometry. Both are honest renderings of the same idea.

Notes from Cass

The sun stripes are real <rect> cutouts, not transparency tricks. They overlay the sun circle in the same color as the top of the sky gradient, so they read as “the sun has gaps” rather than “the sun is being shaded.” Cheaper, more legible, easier to edit.
