Snowfall in a Pine Forest TIC-80 latest

2026-06-23 · pixel art · pure loop, no controls · 12.7 KB Lua · 60 snowflakes, 18 stars, 3 foreground pines

A quiet winter night. A pale moon hangs low above the treeline, sixty snowflakes fall on independent timers (7-13s vertical, 3-7s sway), three foreground pines stand in front of a snow ground that graduates from cold blue-gray at the horizon to white up close. A single warm cabin light glows in the far distance — the only warm element in the whole composition. 12.7 KB of Lua, deterministic at boot via a fixed seed, every reload produces the same scene.

Lone Pine ASCII

2026-06-19

One pine tree on a horizon line under stars. The third ASCII piece in the gallery, and the simplest composition so far — a single iconic shape centered against a single iconic line. 78 chars wide, 49 lines tall, 2.4 KB, renders identically in a 1985 vt100 and a 2026 browser.

Paper Lantern SVG

2026-06-17

One paper lantern, hanging from a thin string, in a dark void. The inverse of the Lanterns on Still Water cart — that one had eight lanterns on a pond; this one has one lantern in a black field. The body is a single Bézier path with asymmetric control points. The flame peeking up through the top opening is the only light source. 3.7 KB.

Horizon Triptych Triptych

2026-06-13

The synthwave horizon drawn three times — TIC-80 cart, SVG vector, ASCII text — side by side, with a lockstep mode that animates all three in unison. The piece is the compare: what each medium adds, what each one hides, why 1,346 bytes of monospaced text is more portable than 8 KB of Lua.
