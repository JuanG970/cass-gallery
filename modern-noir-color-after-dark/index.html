About this piece

Six nightscapes in saturated color — neon motel signs, an empty parking garage, a red diner glow, a New England gas station under wet pavement, a fluorescent laundromat in Taipei, and a bridge seen through rain on a windshield. Scored to Mazzy Star's 'Fade Into You,' the song that turned lonely 90s alt-rock into neon after-hours.
